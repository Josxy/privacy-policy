Privacy Policy for Yes! - Your Entertainment Space

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

Hello!

We're Yes!, and we believe in being straightforward about how we handle your information. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

What Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: We collect your email when you create an account. This is how you log in and how we can contact you if needed.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We track how you interact with Yes!, including:
    • Which games you play
    • How long you play each game
    • Which games you save to your favorites
    • General app usage patterns

We collect this data to understand what games people enjoy and to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the app: Let you log in, save favorites, and use Yes!
  • Improve our service: Understand which games are popular and how to make the app better
  • Share insights with game studios: We share anonymous statistics (like view counts, play counts, and average play duration) with game developers. We never share information about individual users or which specific users played which gamesâ€”only aggregate numbers.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud service provider. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Retention

We keep your account information and usage data for 3 months after your last activity. If you delete your account, we delete your data immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account: You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. When you do, we permanently delete your email and all associated data.
  • Contact us: If you have questions about your data, reach out to us at yusufsavasx@gmail.com

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your personal information to anyone
  • We don't use advertising trackers
  • We don't share your email address with third parties
  • We don't track you across other websites or apps

Children's Privacy

Yes! is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify you through the app or via email.

International Users

Yes! is operated from Turkey. By using our app, you consent to your information being transferred to and processed in Turkey.
